tBodyAcc-mean()-XYZ
  Average value of Body Acceleration means in x,y,z directions  (g)

tBodyAccJerk-mean()-XYZ

tBodyGyro-mean()-XYZ

tBodyGyroJerk-mean()-XYZ
  Average value of Body Gyro Jerk means in x,y,z directions  (radians/s)
